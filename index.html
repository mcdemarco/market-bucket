About

Lists

Market Bucket classifies your groceries into three lists: now, later, and archived.
(You can rename these lists.)
Checked-off items are automatically archived, or you can rearrange your lists manually.

Social

List sharing powered by the social networking platform app.net.
To create or access grocery lists, you will need a (free) app.net account.

Tags

Market Bucket uses hashtags to further sort your lists--by store, item type, or anything else you choose.
Market Bucket stores your hashtags for easy reference; you can edit your hashtag list as well.

Log in with App.net

An App.net account is required to use Market Bucket. You can create an account and sign in by clicking this button.
